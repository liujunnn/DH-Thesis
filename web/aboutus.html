About Our Research Project

Enhancing Digital Cultural Heritage through Ontological Modeling

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Enhancing Digital Cultural Heritage

Project Overview

This research project aims to address the challenges in metadata integration and standardization between different European institutions housing Chinese porcelain collections. By developing unified ontologies and metadata schemas, we facilitate better knowledge exchange and collaboration among cultural institutions.

Our Goals

  • Develop a comprehensive ontology for Chinese porcelain
  • Enhance metadata quality and interoperability
  • Improve accessibility of Chinese porcelain collections
  • Foster collaboration between cultural institutions

Research Process

Data Collection

Gathering metadata from various European museums and cultural institutions holding Chinese porcelain collections.

Ontology Development

Creating a structured ontology framework that captures the semantic relationships between different porcelain attributes.

Metadata Standardization

Establishing common metadata standards to ensure consistency and interoperability across diverse collections.

Knowledge Mapping

Visualizing relationships between different porcelain artifacts, their origins, and historical contexts.

Digital Implementation

Developing digital tools and platforms to facilitate access to the structured knowledge about Chinese porcelain.

Collaboration & Dissemination

Engaging with cultural institutions and researchers to promote the use of the ontology and foster collaboration.
